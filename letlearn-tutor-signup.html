1. Agreement to Terms

By submitting a tutor application on LetLearn, you ("the Tutor") agree to be legally bound by these Tutor Terms & Conditions. If you do not agree, you must not complete the registration process. These terms govern your relationship with LetLearn NG as an independent service provider.

2. Tutor Status & Independence

You are an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, or partner of LetLearn NG. You are solely responsible for your own taxes, insurance, and liabilities. LetLearn acts as a marketplace connecting you with students and does not direct or control how you deliver your sessions.

3. Eligibility & Identity Verification

You must be at least 18 years of age. You must provide accurate personal information and valid identity documents (NIN, certificate, government-issued ID) during registration. Submitting false or fraudulent documents will result in permanent removal from the platform and may be reported to relevant authorities.

4. Profile Accuracy

Your tutor profile — including qualifications, subjects, experience, and rates — must be accurate and kept up to date. LetLearn reserves the right to remove, suspend, or request corrections to any profile that contains misleading or inaccurate information.

5. Session Conduct & Quality Standards

You are expected to be punctual, professional, and prepared for every session. Repeated cancellations, no-shows, or poor student feedback may result in profile suspension. LetLearn may conduct performance reviews based on booking history and student ratings.

6. Tutor Performance Tiers

LetLearn operates a performance tier system: Rising Star (new tutors), Verified Pro (4.0+ rating, 10+ sessions), Elite Tutor (4.7+ rating, 50+ sessions). Higher tiers receive priority placement in search results and promotional features. Tier status is reviewed monthly.

7. Fees & Payments

LetLearn charges a platform service fee on each completed booking. The fee percentage will be communicated to you at onboarding and may be updated with 14 days' notice. Payments are processed after session completion and transferred within 3–5 business days. LetLearn is not liable for delays caused by banking infrastructure.

8. Cancellation Policy

Tutors must give at least 12 hours' notice for session cancellations. Cancellations within 12 hours may result in a penalty fee deducted from future earnings. Three or more late cancellations within a 30-day period may lead to profile suspension.

9. Prohibited Conduct

Tutors must not: solicit students to pay outside the LetLearn platform; share students' personal contact information without consent; engage in discriminatory, abusive, or inappropriate behaviour; provide false academic credentials or impersonate another person; or use the platform for any unlawful purpose.

10. Content & Intellectual Property

Any materials, notes, or content you create for sessions remain your intellectual property. However, any reviews, ratings, and session metadata generated on the LetLearn platform belong to LetLearn NG and may be used for platform improvement and marketing.

11. Suspension & Termination

LetLearn may suspend or permanently remove your account for violations of these terms, sustained low performance (below 3.0 rating over 20+ sessions), fraudulent activity, or any conduct harmful to students or the platform. You may voluntarily deactivate your account at any time by contacting support.

12. Limitation of Liability

LetLearn is not liable for any loss of earnings, damage to reputation, or other indirect damages arising from your use of the platform. Our total liability to you shall not exceed platform fees paid in the 3 months preceding any claim.

13. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall be resolved through good-faith negotiation, failing which through arbitration in Lagos State.

1. Data We Collect from Tutors

During registration and ongoing use, we collect: full name, date of birth, state and address, email, phone number, NIN, qualification certificates, government-issued ID, live photo, social media links, subjects taught, session rates, and booking/performance history.

2. Why We Collect This Data

Your data is used to: verify your identity and credentials; display your profile to potential students; process bookings and payments; generate performance insights and tier classifications; send you platform notifications and alerts; and improve platform quality and safety.

3. KYC & Identity Documents

Identity documents (NIN, certificates, government ID) are collected for Know Your Customer (KYC) verification only. These are reviewed by authorised LetLearn admin staff and stored encrypted. They are never shared with students or third parties without your consent, except where required by law.

4. Profile Visibility

Your public tutor profile — including name, photo, subjects, rating, and bio — is visible to all users of the LetLearn platform. Your contact details (phone, email, social links) are only shared with students who have a confirmed booking with you.

5. Performance Data

Booking history, session completion rates, student ratings, and subject demand data may be used internally for platform analytics, tutor tier classification, and anonymised marketing content (e.g. "Top Maths tutors in Lagos this month"). Your name will not be used in marketing without your explicit consent.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with: students you're matched with (contact details only after confirmed booking); payment processors for earnings transfers; regulatory bodies if required by Nigerian law; and trusted technical partners who operate our platform under strict data agreements.

7. Data Security

All data is stored with AES-256 encryption. Our platform uses HTTPS for all data transfers. Access to tutor KYC documents is restricted to authorised admin only. We conduct regular security audits to protect your information.

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained for the duration of your active tutor account plus 12 months after deactivation (for dispute resolution purposes). KYC documents are deleted within 90 days of account closure unless legally required to retain them longer.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; request corrections to inaccurate data; request deletion of your account and associated data; opt out of marketing communications at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

10. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and analytics cookies to understand platform usage. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. You will be notified by email of any material changes. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact the LetLearn Team at [email protected] or call/WhatsApp 09042427548.
