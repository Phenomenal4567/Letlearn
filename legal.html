Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LetLearn NG uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our platform. By using LetLearn, you consent to our use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, improve performance, and understand how users interact with the site. LetLearn uses both session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain for a set period).

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These are required for the platform to function properly. They enable you to stay logged in, remember your session, and use core features like scholarship search and booking. You cannot opt out of these.

  • Session authentication token
  • Admin session cookie (for admin dashboard access)
  • User preferences (state, academic level)

Performance Cookies

These help us understand how users interact with LetLearn so we can improve the platform. Data collected is anonymous and aggregated.

  • Page load times and performance metrics
  • Most searched subjects and states
  • Feature usage patterns

Functional Cookies

These remember your choices to give you a more personalised experience.

  • Saved search filters (level, state)
  • Last viewed scholarship categories
  • Mobile menu preference

3. Third-Party Cookies

LetLearn may use limited third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts: Used to load our typography. Google may set performance cookies.
  • Cloudflare: Used for security and performance. Cloudflare sets cookies for bot protection and load balancing.
  • Supabase: Our database provider may use session cookies for authentication.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies from ad networks.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here's how for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Chrome on Android: Settings → Site Settings → Cookies
⚠️ Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent some features of LetLearn from working correctly, including login and scholarship search.

5. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Authentication cookies: Expire after 30 days of inactivity
  • Preference cookies: Stored for up to 1 year
  • Admin session: Cleared when the browser tab is closed

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as we add new features or change our service providers. Check this page periodically for updates. Continued use of LetLearn after updates means you accept the changes.
