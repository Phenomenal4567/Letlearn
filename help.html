Help Centre

How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions about scholarships, tutors, and your LetLearn account.

🔍
👤 Account & Registration
Is LetLearn free to use? ▼
Yes! Creating an account and searching for scholarships on LetLearn is completely free. There are no hidden charges for students. Tutors set their own rates which are visible before booking.
How do I create an account? ▼
Click "Join Free" on the homepage or visit letlearn.ng/signup. Fill in your name, email, state, academic level, and password. You can also sign up quickly with your Google account.
I forgot my password. What do I do? ▼
On the Sign In page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a password reset link. If you signed up with Google, use the "Continue with Google" option instead.
Can I change my state or academic level after signing up? ▼
Yes. Go to your profile settings after logging in and update your state and academic level at any time. Your scholarship recommendations will automatically refresh based on your new details.
🎓 Scholarships
How does the AI scholarship search work? ▼
Our AI analyses your search query, state, and academic level to surface the most relevant Nigerian scholarship opportunities in seconds. Results are generated fresh for every search, so you always see current and varied opportunities.
Are the scholarships on LetLearn real and up to date? ▼
Yes. Our AI is trained on verified Nigerian scholarship data and is regularly updated. However, we always recommend verifying deadlines and requirements directly on the official scholarship provider's website before applying.
How do I apply for a scholarship I found? ▼
Click "Quick Apply" on any scholarship card. Fill in your name, email, and academic level. We'll email you the official application link and requirements for that scholarship directly.
Can I search for scholarships by state? ▼
Yes! Use the state filter on the scholarship search to see scholarships specific to your state, as well as nationwide opportunities available to all Nigerian students.
Are there scholarships for JAMB candidates? ▼
Yes. Select "SS3 / JAMB" in the Academic Level filter to see scholarships and grants specifically available for pre-university and JAMB candidates across Nigeria.
👨‍🏫 Tutors & Bookings
How are tutors verified on LetLearn? ▼
Every tutor goes through a 7-step application process including credential verification, identity check, subject assessment, and manual approval by our admin team. Tutors only appear on the platform after full approval.
Can I book an online session from any state? ▼
Yes. Most of our tutors offer online sessions via Google Meet or similar platforms, available to students in all 36 states. In-person sessions depend on the tutor's location.
What happens after I book a session? ▼
After booking, you'll receive a confirmation and the tutor will contact you on your provided phone number to confirm the session time and share the meeting link or location details.
My subject isn't listed. Can I still find a tutor? ▼
Yes! Scroll to the bottom of the Tutors section on the homepage and use the "Request a Tutor" form. Enter your subject, state, and phone number and we'll match you with a verified tutor within 24 hours.
How much do tutors charge? ▼
Each tutor sets their own hourly rate. Rates on LetLearn typically range from ₦2,200 to ₦4,500 per hour. The rate is always shown clearly on the tutor's card before you book.
💳 Payments
How do I pay my tutor? ▼
Currently, payment is arranged directly between the student and tutor after booking confirmation. In-app payment processing via Paystack is coming in our next update.
Does LetLearn take a commission from tutors? ▼
Not currently. Tutors keep 100% of their earnings during our launch phase. A small platform fee may be introduced in future as we add payment processing features.
⚙️ Technical
The scholarship search isn't loading. What should I do? ▼
This can happen on slow connections. Try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection. The search works on 3G and above. If it continues, contact us at letlearn@gmail.com.
Does LetLearn work on mobile? ▼
Yes. LetLearn is designed mobile-first and works on all smartphones including Android and iOS. No app download is needed — it runs directly in your browser.
Is my personal data safe on LetLearn? ▼
Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and never sold to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
