📱 Social Media Content Strategy
Use your admin data as a goldmine for TikTok & Instagram marketing content.
💡
Why This Works
Every booking, every search, every rating on LetLearn tells a story. Turn that data into social proof — the most powerful form of marketing for Nigerian students on TikTok and Instagram.
▶️
TikTok Strategy
Short-form video content ideas
📹 Script Idea #1 — "Did You Know?"
"This week, over 2,000 students in Lagos searched for a Maths tutor on LetLearn. Here are the Top 3 who are ACTUALLY changing grades 👇 [cut to tutor profile snippets]"
📹 Script Idea #2 — Transformation Story
"Student went from D7 to A1 in Chemistry in 6 weeks using LetLearn. Here's exactly how 🧪✨ [student testimonial + tutor intro]"
📹 Script Idea #3 — Trending Subjects
"POV: You live in Lagos and every other student is panicking about WAEC Maths 😭 — meanwhile LetLearn students already booked their tutors 2 weeks ago 💅"
💡 Post between 6–9 PM WAT (Nigeria prime time). Use #WAEC2026 #JAMB2026 #NaijaStudents for organic reach.
